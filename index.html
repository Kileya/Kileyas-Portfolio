About Me

Hi, I’m Kileya Johnson. I am a freshman in high school in Chesterfield County. I currently have over 40 hours of volunteering and I plan to start working paying jobs soon.

I currently go to school at the Meadowbrook Academy for Developing Entrepreneurs, or M.A.D.E. My favorite subjects are currently Biology, Entrepreneurship, and Spanish. This year I have had an AB Honor Roll all year with the exception of the first marking period. I have participated in DECA, an organization that prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality and management in high schools and colleges. I have also worked with and in the 21st street church of god food bank. I have also worked at starting my own volunteering opportunity for high school students with a friend of mine. I am currently in the Girls For a Change ambassador program.

College plans: With the programs that I am in, I will be graduating high school with an Associates Degree. After graduating, I would like to go to college to major in Forensic Science and minor in business. I would like to apply to George Mason and VCU for their programs and I’m still looking at others.

I plan on starting my own business during high school and continuing/expanding it throughout my college years. Once I graduate college, I would like to find a job in the justice system as a Forensic Scientist. I plan on staying in Virginia, and maybe moving away from Chesterfield a little bit.
